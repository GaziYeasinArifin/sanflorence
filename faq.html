The apps are free to download, with optional subscriptions or in-app purchases that unlock the full AI features. You can try the basics before deciding to subscribe.

All purchases are processed by Apple through your App Store account. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends. You can manage or cancel anytime in Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us, per Apple's refund policy. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.
